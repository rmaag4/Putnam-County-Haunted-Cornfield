Directions

FORMERLY KNOWN AS SCHMITZ HAUNTED CORNFIELD

Get Directions on Google Maps

From Lima:

Take State Route 65 North toward Ottawa and turn left on Road M at the Haunted Cornfield sign. Go West on Road M about 3 miles, turn left on Road 12 and the Haunted Cornfield will be on your right.

From Findlay:

Take State Route 224 West through Ottawa. Turn left onto Road 12 outside of Glandorf at the Haunted Cornfield sign, continue until you cross Road M and you will see the Haunted Cornfield on your right.

From the West:

Take State Route 224 East toward Ottawa and turn right onto Road 12 before Glandorf at the Haunted Cornfield sign, continue until you cross Road M and you will see the Haunted Cornfield on your right.
