Frequently Asked Questions

FORMERLY KNOWN AS SCHMITZ HAUNTED CORNFIELD

How much does it cost?

The cost of admission is $20 for everyone. That gets you into the Haunted Cornfield, 2 levels of the barn, and the trailers of terror.

How long does it take to go through?

Depending on the speed of your group, it should take you around 35 minutes to complete the trail through the cornfield and the barn.

How old do you have to be to go through?

There are no age limits for Putnam County's Haunted Cornfield, but it is not recommended for small children. It is up to the parent to decide whether or not to send their child through. Remember, there are NO REFUNDS.
